The City of Calgary

Calgary is one of Canada's wealthiest, cleanest and most liveable cities. Like Edmontonian, Calgary residents enjoy a lower cost of living, a good job market and low taxes.Calgarians also have the lowest provincial personal income tax rate in Canada at just 10 per cent.

Divisded of Calgary

The dividing line between east and west is Centre Street in the north, and roughly Macleod Trail in the south. Roads with the suffix Avenue run east-west, and roads with the suffix Street run north-south.

Calgary is divided into four quadrants:

  • Northeast (NE)
  • Northwest (NW)
  • Southeast (SE)
  • Southwest (SW)

Culture of Calgary

Calgary's culture is as diverse as our population, finding expression across the city through art, food, fashion, dance, film, festivals, cultural celebrations, literary programs, walking and cycling events, community theatre, powwows and even pancake breakfasts. There are over 120 languages spoken in Calgary.

Religion of Calgary
